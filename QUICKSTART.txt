              🎯 HOW THE GAME WORKS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Computer picks a secret word
2. You guess a word
3. Get similarity score (0-100%)
4. Use feedback to narrow down
5. Find the target word!

Example:
  Target: "happy"
  Guess "joyful" → 85% 🔥 Very close!
  Guess "car"    → 15% ❄️ Try again!
